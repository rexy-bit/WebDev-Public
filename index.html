Home Page

My Personal Library is a simple and intuitive single-page application (SPA) that lets you manage your personal reading list with ease. You can add new books, edit existing entries, delete unwanted ones, and keep track of each book's title, author, genre, reading status, and rating. The app also allows you to search and filter your collection, view helpful statistics such as total books read and average rating, and all your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorageâ€”so no account or internet connection is needed. It's the perfect tool to organize and track your reading journey anytime, anywhere.
