Home Page

Welcome to my Todo List SPA project! This application allows you to easily manage your daily tasks in one place. You can add, view, and organize your tasks, mark them as done or undone, and track useful statistics about your productivity. The interface is designed as a Single Page Application (SPA), meaning you can navigate between pages smoothly without reloading. It's built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and uses localStorage to save your data. Enjoy staying organized!
